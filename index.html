Timeline

2016

2016

WEBBERN conducted 4 events in the cultural fest Riviera'16. WEBBERN conducted its first summit Horizon'16 and introduced participants with different aspects of website development.

2015

2015

WEBBERN conducted 3 events in the cultural fest Riviera'15 and 14 events in the technical fest graVITas'15. WEBBERN took its members on an Industrial Visit to Daimler Chennai, hence introduced students to the Industrial World this year.

2014

2014

WEBBERN conducted 4 events in the cultural fest Riviera'14 and 12 events in the technical fest graVITas'14. This year saw WEBBERN conducting a series of workshops called Tech League and Tronix League.

2013

2013

WEBBERN conducted 4 events in the cultural fest Riviera'13 and 13 events in the technical fest graVITas'13. We also started various outreach programs like Teach For Vellore and IC Bank.

2012

2012

WEBBERN conducted 2 events in the cultural fest Riviera'12 and 10 events in the technical fest graVITas'12. WEBBERN-VITU won the "Best Students' Chapter Award" at WEBBERN National Students' Convention this year.

2011

2011

WEBBERN conducted 5 events in the cultural fest Riviera'11 and 9 events in the technical fest graVITas'11. The National Summit Talk @ Green India (TAGI) was also organised by us on a much larger scale attracting students interested in discussing our role in saving the planet of disastrous consequences.
