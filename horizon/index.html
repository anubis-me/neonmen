19th WEBBERN Convention

WEBBERN-VITU brings the WEBBERN National Students' Convention to VIT University, Vellore! The convention is a forum for all WEBBERN chapters all over the country to come together and exchange scientific and innovative concepts and to highlight the role of students in directing it towards excellence! With more than 500 people participating, and people coming down from the WEBBERN National Headquarters themselves, we are very honoured with this oppurtunity.

Industrial Vist

Every year WEBBERN-VITU conducts Industrial visits for its members and registered students. The last Industrial visit was arranged to Daimler India Commercial Vehicles, Chennai. The students visited units that allow the entities to collaborate on areas such as product development, production, exports, sourcing and optimizing research and sales & market development activities to provide the best of value products to their customers. At the end, a workshop was also organized for the further guidance.
